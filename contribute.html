Ways to contribute

Hey, great you are considering to contribute to ScalaMock!

A good place to start would be our issues page on Github, especially those tagged with "help-wanted".

Remember, ScalaMock is licensed under the permissive MIT license, so your contributions must be under [these terms](https://github.com/paulbutcher/ScalaMock/blob/master/LICENCE).
