Issue tracker

ScalaMock issues can be submitted to our issues page on github.

Stack Overflow

You can also ask questions on Stack Overflow with #scalamock tag - we have subscription for questions tagged "scalamock".

Gitter

While ScalaMock does not have its own chat channel, it is worth asking in the official Gitter Scala channel which is well attended and pretty helpful.
